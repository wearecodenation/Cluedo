How to play

Welcome to Code Nation Cluedo!

First start by clicking "Next Move", your game piece will move to a room and reveal a new clue. These clues are sometimes encrypted, othertimes not - once you have an answer or solution enter it into the box and hit enter, if you're correct you'll be told, otherwise try again!

You can save clues for later by closing the clue, to revisit a saved clue simply click on the room from the "Visited Rooms" list.

Once you've received all of the available clues you'll be given three options, and from there you just have to crack the code and solve the mystery!

Happy hunting, detective.
